our story

Where Exquisite Flavors Meet Elegant Ambiance for an Unforgettable Fine Dining Experience!

Welcome to Aristocrat Caterers At Aristocrat Caterers, Events & Decorators, we believe that every celebration deserves to be unforgettable. Founded in 1988 by Mr. Madan Kumar Bhanja, a visionary entrepreneur from Medinipur, West Bengal, Aristrocrate has grown from humble beginnings into one of Delhi NCR's most trusted names in catering, events, and sweets.

For over three decades, we have been serving families, communities, and organizations with authentic flavors, elegant decor, and heartfelt service. From intimate rituals to grand weddings, from traditional Bengali sweets to trending confections, we bring together taste, tradition, and innovation to create moments that last a lifetime.
